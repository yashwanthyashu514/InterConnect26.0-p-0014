PROBLEM TITLE :
Bridging the industry mentorship gap through interest based Student guidance.

PROBLEM STATEMENT :

Academic institutions provide students with essential theoretical knowledge, but many learners face challenges in translating this knowledge into industry-relevant skills and career outcomes. The absence of a structured mentorship framework often leaves students uncertain about the technologies to learn, projects to build, and career roadmap to follow.

Students with similar career aspirations are rarely grouped into focused communities where they can collaborate, share resources, and learn under the guidance of experienced mentors. This lack of organized mentorship and peer-driven growth results in fragmented skill development, low practical exposure, and insufficient placement readiness.

Hence, there is a need for a centralized mentorship platform that bridges the gap between academic learning and industry expectations

PROPOSED SOLUTION :

The proposed system is a career mentorship and student growth platform designed to connect students, mentors, and peer groups under a single digital ecosystem.

The platform works by allowing students to select their career interests and learning domains, after which they are placed into relevant communities. Dedicated mentors with expertise in those domains are then assigned to guide them through structured learning paths, real-world projects, industry tools, placement preparation, and milestone tracking.

This ensures that students receive:

the right mentorship
the right peer network
the right project exposure
the right placement preparation path

OBJECTIVES OF THE PROJECT :

 * Bridge the gap between academics and industry expectations
 * Provide structured mentorship for career growth
 * Encourage peer learning among like-minded students
 * Support project-based practical learning
 * Improve internship and placement readiness
 * Track student progress through milestones
 * Enable expert-led sessions and domain webinars

KEY FEATURES :

 * Interest-based student grouping
 * Domain-specific mentor allocation
 * Peer-to-peer collaboration spaces
 * Career roadmap suggestions
 * Project milestone tracking
 * Industry expert webinars
 * Placement preparation resources
 * Learning progress dashboard
